The Death Of Rugby

by Matt Lilley

Date
October 17, 2025
Time
6:30 PM
Venue
Good Times Comedy Club
Price

Join Christchurch comedy legend Matt Lilley as he delivers the eulogy rugby never knew it needed. "The Death Of Rugby" is a hilarious yet heartfelt farewell to New Zealand's most sacred institution.

From the glory days of World Cup triumphs to the crushing defeats that broke a nation's heart, Matt Lilley mourns our beloved game with the perfect blend of irreverence and affection.

Come pay your respects to the sport that once united us all.
