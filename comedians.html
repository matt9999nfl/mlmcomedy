Matt Lilley

CEO/Director/Comedian

Matt's big in many ways, big heart, big hair, big hands, but most importantly he brings the big laughs! Matt Lilley is one of Otautahi Comedy's rising stars and brings a sturdy foundation along with his out the gate comedy style that's sure to leave an impact!

Lark Wilson

Creative Director/Comedian

Sophisticated, eloquent and absurd. All words that frequent Lark's vocabulary. Lark Wilson - once described as a "hidden gem" of the New Zealand comedy scene, is one of the county's most exciting and original comedic talents. She will charm you with her idiosyncratic presence and poignant punchlines, all while making a visual statement in her own unique style.
